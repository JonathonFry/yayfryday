My Shopping Haul

Jul 13, 2012
2 minute read

As I mentioned yesterday, here’s my post on what I spent most of my £100 on. I ordered everything online except my jacket, and it all came within 3 days!

First up here’s my Topshop haul. It all came to £45.40 incl the £4 delivery. I managed to get student discount, and the 10% even came off sale items! So those jeans only cost me £9 :)

Clockwise from left: MOTO HIGH WAISTED KRISTEN // SALMON BALLET PUMPS // COTTON LYCRA TUNIC // FLORAL SKIRT // NAILS IN POOL PARTY

I am pleased with everything. My favourite thing has to be the jeans, they fit really well. I blogged already about the nail polish here.

After browsing Topshop I had to check out the ASOS sale. Lots of bargains to be found there! Unfortunately lots of things I wanted weren't available in my size. But I managed to get these jeans in indigo, and they fit perfectly, another great price. ASOS also offers free delivery and student discount (but not on sale items) too!

VERO MODA SKINNY JEANS

Finally, after work I popped in to some shops looking for a jacket. I managed to pick one up at Zara. It wasn't on sale but I really liked it and it feels like it'll last. Plus I needed one as I only really have a winter coat at the moment and seeing as it's July I could really do with something a bit lighter. It cost me £40, eek.

Unfortunately I can't seem to find it online, so here's a pic I took of my shopping haul with the jacket included!

So that's how I managed to spend most of my money... Have you picked out anything in the summer sales?
