Valentine's Baking and Craft Ideas

Feb 08, 2012
3 minute read

It’s less than a week now till Valentine’s Day, which means there’s still a weekend to come for time to make something. It is also my birthday on Saturday which means I will only have Sunday free, although I may have a hangover it will probably be a good cure!  So I’ve had a browse on the internet for craft and baking ideas to make for my boyfriend, and here are my favourites:

[hr]

CUPID'S ARROW CUPCAKES

Source: Bakerella

These red velvet Cupid's Arrow Cupcakes from Bakerella are so cute! The recipe is really clear, with lots of gorgeous photos, and there's a free pdf to create the hearts!

[hr]

BROWNIE HEART CUPCAKES

Source: Martha Stewart

These chocolate Brownie Heart Cupcakes from Martha Stewart look so tasty and so simple to make. My boyfriend loves brownies too, and who doesn't like chocolate?!

[hr]

FLAGGED CUPCAKES

Source: Lorajean's Magazine

These cupcakes from Lorajean's Magazine look so sweet! Just make you're favourite cupcakes and make your own flag toppers using toothpicks and decorate them however you like.

[hr]

PINK VELVET CAKE POPS

Source: lovefromtheoven

These Pink Velvet Cake Pops from love from the oven are a great alternative to cupcakes. I've never made cake pops before, but they are such a good idea and look like they'd be so easy to eat, no wrapper to take off or anything! The recipe looks easy to follow with lots of pictures to help.

[hr]

HEART PANCAKES

Source: Martha Stewart

These Heart Shaped Pancakes from Martha Stewart would be a great idea for Valentine's breakfast. I make pancakes a lot as they are so quick and cheap to make, and these look so yummy!

[hr]

CUPID'S ARROW CUPCAKES

Source: Creature Comforts

How adorable are these Cupid's Arrow Cupcakes from Creature Comforts? Using your own cupcakes, Creature Comforts have created these cupcake toppers and wraps available to download! The detailed instructions also include Cupid's Arrow treat bag toppers too, check them out!

[hr]

POPCORN INVITATION

Source: Eat Drink Chic

This DIY Valentine Popcorn Invitation from Eat Drink Chic looks like a fab idea for a cosy night in. With downloadable pdf's this would be an ideal gift, I would love to make something this pretty!

[hr]

VALENTINE COUPON BOOK

Source: MommybydayCrafterbynight

This Valentine Coupon Book by Mommy by day... Crafter by night is a great cheap idea, and just requires a printer! It contains pre-done pages and blank ones too to make your own!

[hr]

PEANUT-BUTTER-AND-JAM HEART COOKIES

Source: Martha Stewart

These sweet Peanut-Butter-and-Jam Cookies from Martha Stewart look so simple to bake. To make the heart shapes you can just use your fingertips!

[hr]

COFFEE SYRUP

Source: A Beautiful Mess

These homemade coffee syrups from A Beautiful Mess are a great idea for any coffee lovers and would make a great gift for Valentine's day. Hmm I'm going to put the kettle on now...!

[hr]

There are so many good ideas on the internet suiting all budgets! So now I've found my favourites I just need to choose between them... if only I could make everything! So what are your Valentine's plans?

S.
