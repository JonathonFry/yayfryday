We had eaten dinner before going but didn't have time to have dessert so I quickly made some when we got in.

We had bought some strawberries and cream, and we've still got a supply of chocolate from Christmas that we don't really like eating as a bar so I use it for baking with. I decided to make a chocolate ganache which I had made before from the book Jamie's 30-Minute Meals. I just halved the ingredients and adapted it a bit.

[hr]

Chocolate Ganache

It's so quick and simple to make. The ingredients I used were:

  • 100g bar good quality chocolate (I used G&B's Maya Gold which is infused with orange and spices)
  • 150ml single cream
  • A pinch of salt
  • Knob of butter
  • Handful of strawberries for dipping
In Jamie's recipe he uses clementines in the ganache but using this chocolate it wasn't needed. I put all of the ingredients (minus the strawberries) into a heatproof bowl over boiling water to melt it all together.

And it's done! Then just spoon it into whatever you have available and get dipping with whatever you fancy, so tasty!
