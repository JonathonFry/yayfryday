Seedlings

Jul 31, 2012
1 minute read

Yay! Some little seedlings have appeared a week after planting my Gardener’s World Seeds. The basil came up first and can already see I’ll have to do quite a bit of thinning with those. Only 3 of my parsley seeds have sprouted, but that’s better than none! The chives were the slowest to sprout, but now there are quite a few seedlings and they’re growing up fast.

Hopefully they’ll all keep on growing now…

S. xo
