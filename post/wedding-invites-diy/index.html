Wedding Invites

My DIY wedding invites are out the door! Using a photoshop template, a paper trimmer and washi tape, I put together an invite, RSVP and Info card.

Feb 26, 2016
1 minute read

Hooray - our wedding invites should have arrived with everyone by now. I wanted them to be quite simple, and I am pleased with how they came out.

We started by looking online, places like Etsy, and struggled to find anything we loved. Whilst browsing I stumbled on a link to Creative Market and a downloadable photoshop template which looked just what we were looking for (here). We had them printed with Vistaprint on 5x7 premium matte card stock, and went with the free envelopes.

The info and RSVP cards are just 5x7 cards cut in half. All the cards have a grey back, and the invite has an ampersand in Abril Fatface font (which I bookmarked a while back as it looks like a J and S). I made a band with kraft paper to hold together the 3 cards and jazzed up the envelopes a bit with washi tape (purchased here).

4 weeks to go!

S. xo
